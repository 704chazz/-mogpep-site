Products & Quality

How do I verify the purity of my order?

+
Every Mogpep order includes a Certificate of Analysis (CoA) from an independent, accredited third-party laboratory. The CoA confirms purity via RP-HPLC and identity via mass spectrometry. You can also verify your batch at mogpep.com/verify using the QR code on your vial.

How should I store my peptides?

+
Lyophilized (unreconstituted): Store at −20°C in a sealed container, protected from light and moisture. Stable for up to 24 months when stored correctly.

After reconstitution: Store at 2–8°C (standard refrigerator) and use within 28 days. Do not refreeze after reconstitution. Always keep away from direct light.

How do I reconstitute a lyophilized peptide?

+
Use bacteriostatic water (BW) or sterile water for injection. Add the solvent slowly down the inside wall of the vial — do not inject directly onto the powder. Gently swirl (do not shake) until fully dissolved. Calculate your desired concentration based on your research protocol. Always perform reconstitution under sterile conditions using appropriate lab technique.
